Form Attributes are:
value(default value for a field i.e autoentered),
readonly (or) disabled(It locks the form field i.e the user can't able to enter),
maxlength(Maximum length of the field.It is generally used for phone number),
placeholder(lighted text in the form fields),
required(must filled option) ,
autofocus(It always redirect to a common field when the page is refreshed),
autocomplete(It will give suggestions to the usesr about recently entered answers )
